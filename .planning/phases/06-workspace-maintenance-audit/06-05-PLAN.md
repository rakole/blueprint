# Phase 6 Plan 05: Bug Index And No-Fix Closeout

## Goal

Reconcile Phase 6 findings, update the bug index slice row, confirm discovery-only boundaries, and prepare Phase 6 for validation without fixing any workspace or maintenance defects.

## Scope

In scope:
- `docs/bugs/INDEX.md` bug rows, duplicate/related findings, and Phase 6 slice coverage row.
- Any `docs/bugs/BPBUG-###-*.md` files created by Plans 01 through 04.
- Phase 6 summaries for workspace, workstream, cleanup, update, and patch replay slices.
- No-fix boundary verification and Phase 6 closeout summary.

Out of scope:
- Source, manifest, skill, test, build, generated asset, runtime, installed-extension, host-global, workspace, patch-registry, branch, PR, or git-history fixes.
- Dedupe and priority ranking across the full milestone; that belongs to Phase 9.
- Packaging, generated `dist`, hooks, install/smoke, and cross-cut drift; those belong to Phases 7 and 8.

Build the Phase 6 inventory:

- List every bug report with `discovery_phase: 6`.

| T-03 | Validation starts from stale or incomplete evidence. | Include test outcomes, blockers, no-fix status, and next validation command in `06-05-SUMMARY.md`. |
